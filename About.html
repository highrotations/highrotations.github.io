High Rotations is all about music. We know everbody else says it, but this time, we mean it. Constantly scrolling through the bullshit posts of major music publications (or "tastemakers"), it seems like the most important part has been forgotten.

This website is not about us. It's all about the guys and girls around the world writing songs for everyone. Music that we can enjoy. Music that we can connect to. Music that we can dance to and sing from the rooftops.

We're here because we enjoy it too. So at High Rotations, you won't see any ratings either - this isn't a competition, it's simply a celebration of the good stuff.

So here's to no politics, agendas, or bullshit. Here's to the music.
