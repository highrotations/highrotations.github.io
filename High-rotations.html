High Rotation is a monthly feature where each of the writers (and/or friends/special guests) create a ten song playlist of whatever has been stuck in their heads. Whether they're new or old, long-time favourites or just discovered, the songs featured in the playlists are what we've had on high rotation (pun very much intended).
