Catfish And The Bottlement announce sophomore album 'The Ride'
23 March 2016

Following the release of first single Soundcheck, Catfish And The Bottlemen haven't waited around to announce their second album, ‘The Ride’. The boys from the UK achieved critical acclaim around the world with their debut ‘Balcony’, especially here in Australia, where fans packed out shows and songs were put on rotation by Triple J. On first taste, the major global touring by the band has had an impact on the followup album, as first taste Soundcheck reflects these themes of endless travel. This slice of fuzzed out goodness also highlights a natural growth from their first record, with a bit more tinkering and chorus’ that sound even bigger than before.

The Ride' will be available 27 May 2016 through Island Records/Universal Music.
