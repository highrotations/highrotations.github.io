Dune Rats blast away the bullshit with their new single
6 May 2016

Old mates Dune Rats have returned in spectacular fashion with the release of the brand new single Bullshit, a tightly wound anthem loaded with their trademark care free vibes. Co-produced with FIDLARâ€™s Zac Carper, Bullshit is a no nonsense belter that paves the way for a heavier and scrappier sound for the three piece. While not as sun soaked as previous tracks, this single retains a signature energy that leans further towards their punk sensibilities with thrashing drums encouraging classic loud/soft dynamics with the walls of bass and guitar. Topped with ragged vocals howling irreverent refrains, Dune Rats have delivered another classically bratty anthem that you need to shout along with.
