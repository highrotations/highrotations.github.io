Hockey Dad announce debut album with first single So Tired
1 April 2016

Wollongong duo Hockey Dad have shared the details of their debut album ‘Boronia’ with single So Tired, their first studio offering last year’s 7 inch ‘Can’t Have Them’. A tasty slice of surf rock heaven, So Tired features the powerhouse drumming of Billy Fleming and the sunny guitars and vocals of Zach Stephenson, bringing good vibes and sounding bigger than a two piece should. It immediately lets off a bouncing energy that feels like the perfect soundtrack to any wacky adventure one could have, such as the tried and true road trip. Despite the name, So Tired is drenched in the sun, shooing all the clouds away and hinting at all the good things yet to come.

Boronia' will be in your ears 12 August 2016 through Farmer & The Owl.
