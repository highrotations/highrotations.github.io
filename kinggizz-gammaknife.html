King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard announce new album with Gamma Knife
9 March 2016

King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard are a band that hardly need an introduction anymore, renowned for face-melting shows around the globe and a seemingly endless output (seven full length albums since 2012!). 2016 looks to be just as busy for the band, who’ve announced their eighth album ‘Nonagon Infinity’ - an infinite prog rock journey - with Gamma Knife. King Gizz’s prolific nature is only matched by a knack for genre hopping, with the frenzied lead single taking us down a new path reminiscent of ‘I’m In Your Mind Fuzz’ but totally removed from ‘Paper Mache Dream Ballon’. After testing out the new album on the road with GOAT, Gamma Knife is a perfect psychedelic taste of what’s sure to be a otherworldly experience.

‘Nonagon Infinity’ out 29 April 2016 through Flightless Records.
