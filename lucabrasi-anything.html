Luca Brasi share third single Anything Near Conviction from forthcoming LP
7 March 2016

Following the screaming Aeroplane and melodic The Cascade Blues, Tasmanian punks Luca Brasi have released the third single Anything Near Conviction from their forthcoming album ‘If This Is All We’re Going To Be’. Just like the previous two singles, Anything Near Conviction sees the band hone their own edge as guitars weave through both restrained and roaring instrumentation. So far, these singles showcase a refinement in sound that fosters an excitement of what to expect from the forthcoming LP. By pushing this envelope, Luca Brasi continue to carve out their own sound in the wake of what could be their best album yet.

‘Is This All We’re Going To Be’ out 29 April 2016 through Poison City Records.
