Remi drops new single For Good with a corresponding national tour
15 March 2016

The partnership that is Remi and Sensible J have dropped For Good, the first single off of the upcoming album ‘Divas And Demons’. Featuring Sampa The Great on vocal duties, the latest offering from the duo is a slinky number that has an undeniable groove circling around bouncing guitars and spacious keys. The vibe is mirrored by the call and response between Remi and Sampa, who fill the space and add to the lush atmosphere. Topped with scathing words that deconstructs the obsession with social media, Remi and co. manage to keep atop the momentum they have built for themselves.

‘Divas And Demons’ is due out in 2016.
