The Gooch Palms cause more trouble on new single Ask Me Why
5 May 2016

There’s an age old saying that goes “Keep It Simple Stupid”, or something along those lines. Well, if you could actually answer a saying, the answer to this one would be Newcastle’s favourite troublemakers The Gooch Palms, who’ve just dropped their new single Ask Me Why. Like the first single Tiny Inisight, Ask Me Why boasts a more refined sound while still sounding as big and scrappy as ever, due in part to the tongue in cheek lyrics and infectious energy. By keeping it simple, the duo get straight to the point with a audacious track that wastes no time avoiding apologies.

The Gooch Palms’ second album ‘Introverted Extroverts’ will be in your ears 15 June 2016 through Summer Camp Records.
