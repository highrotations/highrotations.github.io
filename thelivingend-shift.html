The Living End announce a triple threat: new album, new single, new tour
21 March 2016

After testing the waters with Monkey, The Living End have announced their upcoming album ‘Shift’ with the release of first single Keep On Running, and the details of a great big Australian tour. Album number seven sounds like a return to stripped back rock and roll for The Living End, and Keep On Running confirms this notion gracefully with fleshed out ideas and lush string arrangements coming together to create a classic. Supported by Bad//Dreems and 131’s, 2016 is set to be a fun year with The Living End.

‘Shift’ will hit the shelves on 13 May 2016 through Dew Process/Universal Music.

The Living End ‘Shift’ tour with Bad//Dreems and 131s:
Friday June 10 - The Tivoli, Brisbane
Saturday June 11 - The Enmore Theatre, Sydney
Thursday June 16 - The Astor, Perth
Friday June 24 - The Forum, Melbourne
Saturday June 25 - The Gov, Adelaide
